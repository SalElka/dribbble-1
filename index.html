Introducing Final.

It's time to say goodbye to breaches

The credit card was broken.

We fixed it.

The next time you hear about a big credit card breach, you can relax.

Final eliminates the hassle associated with a cancelled card, fraud, or theft. Don't waste time re-establishing your payment relationships. Don't worry about missing important bills or about constantly checking your statement.

Final puts you in total payment control

Final let's you give a unique credit card number to every merchant, or a disposable one-time use umber for one-off purchases.

Tired of your monthly yoga pants subscription? Or a two-week "free" trail that lasted four months? Just deactivate that card number. It's that easy.

With Final, you can limit each merchant or subscription to a monthly amount. If the merchant attempts to go over that limit, you recieve a push notification, keeping you in the loop

Final is aligned with you, your goals, and how you buy.

You can limit your ability to spend based on your personal budget, and we will work with you towards building good credit, not bad debt.

Your credit card should work for you, not against you. This is why we've built Final.
